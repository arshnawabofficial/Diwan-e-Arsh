Diwan-e-Arsh

Shayari ka safar mere liye kisi kitaab, mehfil ya kisi ustad se shuru nahi hua… yeh safar shuru hua tha ek ajeeb si bechaini se.

Woh daur aisa tha jab bina wajah dil tez dhadakne lagta tha, haath halka sa kampkapa jaate the, saans bhaari si ho jaati thi, aur samajh nahi aata tha ki aakhir ho kya raha hai. Sab kuch theek hote hue bhi andar ek khalipan tha… jaise dil kisi anjaani baat ka bojh utha raha ho.

Kabhi lagta tha sab theek hai… aur phir achanak dil ghbrane lagta tha. Khud se hi ajeeb si doori mehsoos hone lagi thi, aur dheere dheere yeh bechaini ek khamosh si aadat ban gayi.

Logon ke beech rehkar bhi tanhai mehsoos hoti thi, raat der tak neend nahi aati thi, aur khayal itne badh jaate the ki kabhi kabhi khud par hi hansi bhi aati — yeh kaisa pagalpan hai… jo samajh bhi nahi aata aur chhootta bhi nahi.

Phir ek din yun hi kalam haath mein aa gayi… koi iraada nahi tha shayari likhne ka, bas dil mein jo chal raha tha, use likhne ki koshish ki. Pehla lafz likhte waqt haath sach mein kampkapa gaya tha, jaise dil apni baat kehne se ghabra raha ho.

Mujhe laga shayad yeh likhna ek raasta ban jaayega — bechaini se door jaane ka, khud se bhaagne ka, ya shayad thoda sa sukoon paane ka.

Magar dheere dheere samajh aaya… shayari bechaini se bhaagne ka zariya nahi bani, balki usne un ehsaason ko aur gehra kar diya.

Jo dard pehle sirf mehsoos hota tha, shayari ne usse lafzon mein utaar diya — aur jab lafz ban kar woh saamne aaye, toh ehsaas aur bhi saaf, aur bhi gehra ho gaya.

Yeh ek ajeeb sa silsila ban gaya… jitna likhta gaya, utna hi andar utarta gaya, jitna samajhta gaya, utna hi dard gehra hota gaya.

Kabhi kabhi lagta tha yeh shayari nahi… ek khamosh sa pagalpan hai, jo har raat jagata hai, har khamoshi mein kuch dhoondhta hai, aur har ehsaas ko lafzon mein badalne ki zid karta hai.

Phir dheere dheere ek aur ehsaas paida hua — is dard se bhaagne ki koshish kam hone lagi… aur is dard mein rehne ki aadat si ho gayi.

Aaj bhi bechaini hoti hai, aaj bhi khayal rukte nahi, magar ab in sab mein ek ajeeb sa sukoon milta hai. Jaise dard bhi apna ho gaya ho… jaise tanhai bhi saath nibhaane lagi ho.

Ab kabhi kabhi lagta hai — shayad yeh dard hi meri shayari ki rooh hai, aur yeh pagalpan hi meri pehchaan.

Main dard se door nahi bhaaga… maine usse mehsoos kiya, usse likha, aur dheere dheere usi mein jeena seekh liya.

Ab is bechaini mein bhi ek lehrahat hai, is khamoshi mein bhi ek awaaz hai, aur is dard mein bhi ek ajeeb sa maza hai.

Shayari mere liye ab shauk nahi rahi… yeh ek safar ban chuki hai — bechaini se alfaaz tak, pagalpan se samajh tak, aur dard se shayari tak. Aur shayad meri shayari ki asli pehchaan bhi yahi hai — main sukoon ki talash mein likhna shuru kiya tha… magar ab is dard mein hi sukoon milne laga hai.
