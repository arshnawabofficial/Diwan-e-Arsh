Rubai

Dil ne jo chaha woh fasana na hua
Waqt ka dariya bhi rawana na hua
Hum dhoondte rahe khudi ko umr bhar
Aur khud se milne ka zamana na hua.

Rait pe likhi hui taqdeer the hum
Lehron ki bas ek si tasveer the hum
Duniya ne samjha humein mitti ka khilona —
Toote toh jaana ki zanjeer the hum
