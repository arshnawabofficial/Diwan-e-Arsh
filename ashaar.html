Ashaar

Short couplets & fragments

Mere chehre prr muskurahat ka parda chadha hai,
Mera Imaan sbko chehra dikhane ki ijazat nhi deta

Hum Mussanif hai janab muft me kya arbo me nhi bikte,
Hum lafzon ke kimat batate hai bazaaro me nhi bikte.

Abb to bss naam me hi Arsh hai
asl manzil hamari bhi farsh hai.
